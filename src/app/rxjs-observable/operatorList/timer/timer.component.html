    What is timer in RxJS?

    timer is a creation operator that emits a number after a specified delay, and—if given a second argument—continues emitting sequential numbers at regular intervals. Think of it as a more flexible combination of setTimeout and setInterval.

    Interview Answer Template:

    timer in RxJS is used to create an observable that emits after a specific delay or after a delay and then continues to emit values at regular intervals. It's useful for delayed execution, retry logic, and polling with a delay. Unlike interval, it gives more control over the initial delay and emission frequency.

    Syntax:

    timer(dueTime: number, period?: number): Observable<number>

    Use Cases:

    • Delayed execution (similar to setTimeout).
    • Polling with an initial delay and then periodic emissions.
    • Implementing retry logic with a backoff delay.
